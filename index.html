Region

In the outskirts of Larissa city and more especially in the Alkazar park area, Burger Time, the best buger house of the city, is waiting for you! After a walk in the shady roads of Alkazar what would it better from making a stop at Burger Time and enjoy a tasteful burger?

A Unique Experience

We are an authentic Burger House where our chefs make their best to create a memorable gastronomic experience for you. Our fresh ingredients are cooked harmonically together and our goal is to satisfy our customers by providing to them high quality food services.


Cuisine

A wide range of plates can be found at Burger Time. Although, we strongly recommend our special dishes. The traditional burger is the ideal choice for someone who loves good food but hesitates to try a new plate. Vegeterian burger is going to satisfy our vegeterian customers and double burger is ideal for really hungry customers!
